How Organic Vegetables Is Benefecial For Our Earth

Eating organic reduces the amount of chemicals in the diet, namely persistent pesticides. The use of insecticides, fungicides, fertilizers and herbicides is strictly controlled in the production of organic foods. Organic farming produces healthy food without the use of toxic pesticides. While some organic farmers use pesticides, they are mostly derived from natural substances.



These natural pesticides must be registered for organic production. The National Organic Standards Board, or NOSB, and the National Organic Program, or NOP maintain a list of materials approved for use in organic production. This list is compiled with input from farmers, business owners, consumers and the public. The general rule for the national list is that natural materials are allowed and synthetic materials are prohibited, but there are some exceptions. Once the NOSB and NOP add a material to the national list, third-party organizations such as the Organic Materials Review Institute evaluate new products to ensure they are compliant.This is a process involving hundreds of technical experts. Learn more about this process from the Rodale Institute. Approved natural pesticides can only be used when other pest control methods are unsuccessful.
